Overview

Cheapstay ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website cheapstay.co. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our service, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Cheapstay, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The short version: We collect minimal data to run the service. We don't sell your personal information to anyone. We earn revenue through referral commissions from booking platforms when you click a link and complete a booking.

Data We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

Information you provide directly:

  • Search queries — destination, hotel name, dates, and number of guests you enter when searching
  • Settings you configure on the site (e.g. cashback rates, affiliate preferences), stored locally in your browser

Information collected automatically:

  • Log data: your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring page, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit
  • Usage data: how you interact with search results and which links you click
  • Cookie data: small text files placed on your device by our site or third-party services (see Cookies section)

We do not collect your name, email address, payment information, or account credentials. No registration is required to use Cheapstay.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve the hotel price comparison service
  • Understand which searches and destinations are most popular so we can improve results
  • Track affiliate link clicks for commission reporting purposes
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or excessive automated requests
  • Monitor site performance and fix technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations where required

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, and we do not build individual user profiles.

Cookies & Tracking

Cheapstay uses cookies and similar technologies. A cookie is a small file stored on your device that helps us recognise your browser on future visits and remember your preferences.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. which pages are visited, how long sessions last). Data is aggregated and anonymised.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Set when you click a booking link. These allow the booking platform to attribute your reservation to Cheapstay for commission purposes. They do not contain personal information beyond the click event.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality. Your browser's help documentation explains how to manage cookie preferences.

Third-Party Services

Cheapstay links to third-party booking platforms including Agoda, Booking.com, and others. When you click a booking link and visit one of these platforms, you are subject to that platform's own privacy policy and terms of service. We encourage you to review their policies before completing a booking.

We participate in affiliate programmes operated by these booking platforms. These programmes use cookies and tracking pixels to record that a visit originated from Cheapstay. The information shared with affiliate programmes is limited to click-level data (a unique session identifier and timestamp) and does not include personally identifiable information.

We may use third-party analytics services (such as privacy-focused analytics tools) to help us understand site usage. These services are configured to anonymise IP addresses and not share data with advertising networks.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the site (e.g. hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Cheapstay or its users
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and others):

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data, such as analytics processing
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@cheapstay.co. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain log and usage data for a maximum of 12 months, after which it is anonymised or deleted. Search query data is not linked to individual users and is retained in aggregate form for service improvement purposes. Settings stored in your browser (localStorage) remain until you clear your browser data.

Children's Privacy

Cheapstay is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of Cheapstay after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
