About Cheapstay

Hotel prices, simplified.

We believe finding a great hotel deal shouldn't require hours of comparison shopping.

Cheapstay is a hotel price comparison tool built for travellers who want the lowest rate — without the hassle. We search across top booking platforms like Agoda, Booking.com, and more in real time, so you can instantly see where your next stay is cheapest and book with a single click.

We started Cheapstay because we were frustrated. Finding the best hotel price used to mean opening five tabs, comparing currencies, factoring in hidden fees, and still wondering if you'd missed a better deal somewhere. We fixed that. Every search on Cheapstay surfaces the lowest available prices across multiple platforms in seconds.

Cheapstay doesn't charge you anything. We earn a small referral commission from booking platforms when you click through and complete a reservation — at no extra cost to you. That's how we keep the lights on and the service free.
